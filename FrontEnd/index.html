Bienvenido a España

Respira el arte y el sabor

Spain, located in southwestern Europe, is a vibrant blend of ancient heritage and modern life. Bordered by France and Portugal and surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean, it offers a landscape as diverse as its culture — from sunbaked coasts to snowy peaks and bustling cities.

Currency :

Euro (€)

Official Language :

Spanish (Castilian); regional languages include Catalan, Galician, and Basque.

Religion:

Predominantly Roman Catholic, though Spain is increasingly secular and tolerant.

Time Zone:

Central European Time (CET), UTC +1
