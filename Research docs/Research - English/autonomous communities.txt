🏞️ 5. Regions, Cities, & Cultural Highlights (Integrated Version)
🌅 A. Andalusia – The Soul of Spain

Major Cities: Seville, Granada, Córdoba

Culture:
Flamenco origin, Moorish architecture, bullfighting, vibrant street life.

Festivals:

Feria de Abril (Seville) – Colorful fair with dancing and horses

Semana Santa (Holy Week processions)

Festival de las Cruces (Córdoba)

Cuisine:
Gazpacho, Salmorejo, Jamón Ibérico, Tapas, Sherry wine.

Must-Visit:
Plaza de España, Alhambra, Mezquita-Catedral, Real Alcázar, Albayzín.

🌊 B. Catalonia – Modernism & Mediterranean Coast

Major Cities: Barcelona, Girona

Culture:
Gaudí’s surreal architecture, Catalan independence pride, seaside art scene.

Festivals:

La Mercè (Barcelona) – Citywide cultural fest

Sant Jordi’s Day (Book & Rose festival)

Castellers (Human towers tradition)

Cuisine:
Pa amb Tomàquet, Escudella, Crema Catalana, Cava (sparkling wine).

Must-Visit:
Sagrada Família, Park Güell, Gothic Quarter, Girona Cathedral.

🏛️ C. Madrid & Central Spain – The Beating Heart

Major Cities: Madrid, Toledo, Segovia

Culture:
Royal heritage, classical art, and café life.

Festivals:

San Isidro (Madrid) – Music, dance & bullfights

Virgen del Pilar (Zaragoza nearby)

Cuisine:
Cocido Madrileño, Bocadillo de Calamares, Churros con Chocolate, Rioja wine.

Must-Visit:
Prado Museum, Royal Palace, Retiro Park, Alcázar of Segovia.

🥘 D. Valencia – The City of Arts & Paella

Major Cities: Valencia, Alicante

Culture:
Mediterranean energy, innovation, and artistic design.

Festivals:

Las Fallas – Sculptures, fireworks & fire parade

La Tomatina (Buñol) – Tomato-throwing festival

Cuisine:
Paella Valenciana, Horchata, Fideuà, Turrón (almond nougat).

Must-Visit:
City of Arts & Sciences, Oceanogràfic, Santa Bárbara Castle.

🏔️ E. Basque Country – Rugged Coast & Culinary Heaven

Major Cities: San Sebastián, Bilbao

Culture:
Euskara language, green hills, and avant-garde art.

Festivals:

Semana Grande (San Sebastián) – Fireworks & concerts

Tamborrada Drum Festival (Donostia)

Cuisine:
Pintxos (mini tapas), Bacalao al Pil-Pil, Txakoli (white wine).

Must-Visit:
Guggenheim Museum, La Concha Beach, Old Town Bilbao.

🌄 F. Galicia – The Green Soul of Spain

Major Cities: Santiago de Compostela, A Coruña

Culture:
Celtic heritage, pilgrimage traditions, misty coasts.

Festivals:

Festa do Apóstolo (Santiago) – Pilgrim celebrations

Semana Grande (A Coruña)

Cuisine:
Pulpo a la Gallega, Empanada Gallega, Albariño wine, seafood platters.

Must-Visit:
Santiago Cathedral, Tower of Hercules, Plaza del Obradoiro.

🏖️ G. Canary Islands – Eternal Springtime

Main Islands: Tenerife, Gran Canaria

Culture:
Volcanic landscapes, island festivals, and tropical music.

Festivals:

Carnival of Santa Cruz de Tenerife – Second largest in the world

Fiesta de San Juan – Bonfires & beaches

Cuisine:
Papas Arrugadas with Mojo, Gofio, tropical fruits, local rum.

Must-Visit:
Mount Teide, Maspalomas Dunes, Loro Parque, Vegueta Old Town.
